Instructions

PC:
  • Click on a planet to zoom in on it.
  • Drag the mouse to rotate the view around the planets.
  • Scroll to zoom in or out for a better view of the solar system.
  • Right-click + Drag to pan across the scene.
Mobile:
  • Tap on a planet to zoom in on it.
  • Pinch with two fingers to zoom in or out.
  • Swipe to rotate the view around the planets.
Simulation Speed Control:
  • Use the slider to adjust the speed of the simulation. A lower value slows down the orbits, while a higher value speeds them up.
Toggle Labels:
  • This button shows or hides the names of the planets and celestial objects in the simulation.
Toggle Orbits:
  • This button shows or hides the orbital paths of the planets, making it easier to focus on the celestial bodies themselves.
